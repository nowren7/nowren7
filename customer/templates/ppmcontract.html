  • Driver Age Restriction:Drivers under the age of 21 are prohibited from operating Yaldi cars.

  • Drained Battery:A fee of AED 100 plus the actual cost of battery replacement applies in cases of drained batteries due to negligence such as leaving headlights or internal lights on.
  • Vehicle Security:An administrative fee of AED 100 applies for unsecured vehicle conditions, including open doors, keys in the ignition, open trunk/boot, open windows, or open sunroof.
  • Driver Age and Authorization Charges:For drivers under 25 years old or unauthorized drivers, an excess amount of AED 500 plus 20% of the total claim will be charged.
